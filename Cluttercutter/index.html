Instructions: Thank you for participating in this brief survey. Your answers are confidential and will only be used for research purposes. There are no right or wrong answers, and your honest feedback is invaluable. This should only take about 3 minutes to complete.

Section 1: Understanding Your Digital Habits

1. On a typical day, how many files (screenshots, documents, downloads, etc.) do you save directly to your computer’s desktop?

2. How often do you feel frustrated or overwhelmed by the number of files on your desktop?

3. When you need to find a specific file on your desktop, how easy is it for you?

Section 2: Gauging Interest in the Solution

4. Imagine a tool that automatically and intelligently moves new files from your desktop into organized folders for you. How interested would you be in using such a tool?

5. For the same tool, which of the following features would be most appealing to you? (Rank in order from 1 to 3, where 1 is the most appealing)

It moves all screenshots to one place automatically
It can run in background
It tracks how many files it cleaned

6. If a tool like this showed you a “Clean Streak” (e.g., “You’ve been clutter-free for 14 days and saved 253 files!”) how likely would you be to share this with friends or on social media?

Section 3: Reflecting and Closing

7. How do you think most people would feel about a tool that cleans their desktop automatically?

8. If you could give this new type of software a name, what would it be?
